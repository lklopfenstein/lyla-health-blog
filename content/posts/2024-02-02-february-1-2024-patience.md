So what do we call this period of time while we wait for brain surgery?   The calm before the storm?  It's strangely quiet now that we're back home from the hospital and there aren't updates to share every day...  We just... wait.  

I read a devotional recently from the Bible app (from Bryce Johnson over at Unpackin' It Ministries) that brought up the fruits of the spirit from Galatians 5.  He read from the amplified translation that reads, "But the fruit of the Spirit [the result of His presence within us] is love [unselfish concern for others], joy, [inner] peace, patience [not the ability to wait, but how we act while waiting], kindness, goodness, faithfulness, gentleness, self-control."

Patience - not the ability to wait, but how we act while waiting.  An excellent reality check for us right now.  We're committed to pointing out the goodness of God to others through all of this, and the season of waiting should be no exception.  Yes, the situation is complete garbage - make no mistake, I hate the circumstance.  However, if we keep our eyes out for the beauty... it's there.  And plenty of it, to boot.
