What a crazy day.  New high score for number of focal seizures in a single day - 106.  One. Hundred. Six. Something for which we were never hoping to hold any kind of world record. 

They've exhausted options for giving her meds in the regular pediatric unit, so we've been moved to the Pediatric ICU to be sedated and given a stronger med (in need of respiratory monitoring) through IV.  She's now sleeping, and they're monitoring seizure activity while she's down.  11 in the past hour, but less intense and shorter.  Perhaps we're moving in the right direction and the last news we received is that they're increasing dosage.  We're looking at it as a bit of a rescue med to get her brain out of its current state of near-nonstop seizures.
