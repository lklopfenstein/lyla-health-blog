I couldn't be more proud of Lyla.  She's been struggling a ton with her daily seizures, but also a good bit of nausea in the evenings.  Yet... her prayers remain so faithful and pure.  "God, whatever you want to do with my body to get more people to know you or know you better."  And "Thank you God - even though I feel yucky every day, You are using it to get people to know you.  So if that's the reason for me having seizures and feeling yucky, then I'm with You."

Man... in a lot of ways she's leading us spiritually.  We try our best for sure, but there's a lot here we didn't teach her - she just seems to "get it".  A faith so pure and genuine.  She continues to just burst into prayer 10-20x a day, and always addresses her prayers to "God, Jesus, and Holy Spirit."  It's amusing and lovely. 

As for this week, we have a night of worship at Core Community Church tonight at 7pm, and her baptism this Sunday.  Can't wait to worship alongside this bright little light and witness a core moment in her faith journey this weekend.  Way to go, little girlie - We're so proud of you on your journey so far, and can't wait to see how God uses you in the future, both near and distant!
